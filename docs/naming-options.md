# Product Naming: Catchy Name Options

## Naming Criteria

- **Memorable:** Easy to remember and spell
- **Descriptive:** Hints at function (shopping + AI + matching)
- **Unique:** Available domain/trademark potential
- **Short:** <10 characters ideal
- **Vibe:** Modern, helpful, not corporate

## My Personal Recommendation

### **Sift**

```
Why:
- 4 letters, easy to remember
- Strong verb = action-oriented
- Implies intelligence (sifting through data)
- Clean, modern feel
- Works as noun and verb ("let Sift find it")
- Not overused

Taglines:
- "Sift finds what fits"
- "Let Sift sift for you"  
- "Sift through the noise"
- "Smart shopping, simplified"

Logo idea:
- Minimalist filter/funnel icon
- Products going in, best one coming out
```

### **Pikk** (Alternative)

```
Why:
- Ultra-short, memorable
- Modern spelling stands out
- Scandinavian minimalism vibe
- Double 'k' is distinctive
- Available in more TLDs likely

Taglines:
- "Pikk your perfect product"
- "The smart pikk"
- "Pikk helps you pick"
```
